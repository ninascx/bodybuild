# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People tracking bodybuilding, weight training, body measurements, daily habits, and weekly progress. They are usually checking in quickly before or after training, reviewing trends, or maintaining a plan over several weeks.

## Product Purpose

LiftLog helps users record daily fitness data, manage training plans, review weekly progress, and keep personal profile targets in sync across local and server-backed storage. Success means the user can trust the data, update common records quickly, and understand what changed without hunting through dense screens.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, encouraging. The interface should feel like a reliable training notebook with enough structure for serious tracking, not a loud fitness campaign.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic gym landing-page aesthetics, decorative gradients, oversized hero sections, gamified clutter, and inconsistent control styles. The app should not feel like a template dashboard or a marketing page wrapped around forms.

## Design Principles

- Make the next action obvious on every screen.
- Treat data safety and clear sync status as part of the user experience.
- Keep repeated controls visually consistent so users can build muscle memory.
- Prefer structured density over empty decorative space.
- Design mobile flows for one-handed check-ins and short sessions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, 44px touch targets for primary controls, reduced-motion friendly transitions, and clear text labels for state, errors, loading, and destructive actions.
